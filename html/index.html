How it works

Tara G! helps businesses to attract and encourage more travelers.
It also helps them to promote their location.

Collect Badge

How to collect badges?

  • Register to the app.
  • Choose destination.
  • Select the specific challenge that you want to take.
  • Collect badge in different areas.
  • Check your rank in leaderboard.
Earn Perks and Rewards

How to earn rewards.

  • Complete Challenges.
  • Exchange your badges to a particular rewards.
  • Go to the business location.
  • Recieve promos, discounts and items from businesses.
